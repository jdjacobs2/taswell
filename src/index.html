WINDSONG is a superb three stateroom, center cockpit sailing yacht in enviable condition, maintained by her owner in turnkey, fully equipped ocean ready cruising condition. She is a totally custom-built blue-water sailing yacht that can be easily handled from the cockpit and cruised by one couple. With a keel depth of 5.5 ft and a mast height of 63 ft WINDSONG easily navigates the ICW.

WINDSONG was a fresh-water boat for her first ten years. During their ten years the second owner sailed the U.S east coast and the Bahamas. The third and present owner also covered the U.S. east coast and the entire Caribbean, often 2 or more times.  WINDSONG has always been a non-smoking, no-pets, and no-shoes below deck yacht!

WINDSONG has been continuously upgraded to where virtually all mechanical, rigging and electronic systems, save the engine and generator, are less 8 years old.  Qualified mechanics have diligently professionally perform all necessary and optional maintenance and upgrades.
